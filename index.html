Vyuham 2K24

Organized by MCA Department

We are pleased to announce that our MCA Department will be hosting the State-Level Technical Symposium, "VYUHAM 2K24," on 18th October 2024. This event aims to provide an excellent platform for undergraduate students from various academic institutions to exchange ideas, explore innovative concepts, and enhance their knowledge. We look forward to your participation and contribution to making this symposium a grand success!

    Debugging

    Starts At - 11:15 A.M

    Instruction

    • In this event Single participant is allowed.
    • You are not allowed to display the output using simple print statements (e.g., System.out.println). The output must be displayed according to the specified instructions provided during the event.
    • The programs will be based on JAVA. Each program will contain errors, and your goal is to identify and fix those errors.
    • Internet access will not be provided, and the use of mobile phones or any other electronic devices is strictly prohibited during the event.
    • Any participant found using unfair means, such as cheating or attempting to bypass the rules, will be immediately disqualified from the event. Please maintain the integrity of the competition.
    • Judges decision will be the final.

    Venue : MCA Lab

    Connection

    Starts At - 01:45 P.M

    Instruction

    • Each team is allowed to have two members.
    • In the first round, 15 pictures will be shown to the participants with 15 seconds given to guess the answer for each question.

    • If the answer is incorrect, the chance will be given to the next team.
    • Participants with the highest score will be selected for the next round.
    • Finally, the judges will determine the winners.
